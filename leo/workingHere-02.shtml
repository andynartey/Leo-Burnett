Development

We are passionate about developing our people.

The IPA has recently awarded us a CPD Gold Standard for our commitment and approach to training for the second year running. This is not a surprise when you consider that last year we delivered an average of 21 hours per person. We have an in-house trainer who delivers group training and one-to-one mentoring and we regularly participate in external industry training courses.

We also offer more innovative development opportunities such as The International Exchange (TIE): Each year we send someone to Brazil for a month to work with a small NGO and local agency to develop a campaign for a charitable cause. Find out more about Alice′s travels and the campaign she developed to help save the street children of Recife at www.alicesadventure.com
