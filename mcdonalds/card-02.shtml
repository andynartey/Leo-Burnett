Our role was to tell good news stories designed to rebuild trust in the brand and re-connect with customers. The activity that followed contributed to one of the most dramatic marketing turnaround stories of recent times.

'Planting' and 'Weather' drove reappraisal of McDonald's by pointing out surprising truths about the food. 'Just passing by' reawakened latent love for McDonald's as a brand by celebrating its role at the heart of
British life.

More people now trust the brand than don't. And to cap it all the ROI of brand TV advertising increased threefold.
