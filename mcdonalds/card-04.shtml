The McDonald's billboard at Piccadilly Circus was designed to make the most of this brilliant, world famous location, turning a poster into a destination in its own right. It's one of our favourite examples of something we call 'mass digital'. Big interactive stuff that happens in the real world.

Still and moving images allow friends to take pictures of themselves interacting with the poster. Nowadays you'll find a distinct change in people's behaviour at Piccadilly Circus, with visitors striking strange poses as they manoeuvre into position for their perfect shot ...
