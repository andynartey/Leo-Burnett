McDonald's exists to provide simple, easy enjoyment.

Our brand communications focus unapologetically on this,
and have two key roles:

The first is to re-build brand trust by celebrating surprising
truths about our food. The second is to re-awaken love for the brand
by celebrating its role at the heart of British life.

The results have been remarkable: millions of people now
trust the brand and feel good about it once again. But most
importantly millions more are visiting every year.
