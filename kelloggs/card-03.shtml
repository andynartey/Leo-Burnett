For decades, Rice Krispies' simple goodness and unique 'snap crackle 'n' pop' have let mothers share in magical moments of pleasure with their kids. But in recent years mums have turned to newer brands either claiming particular health benefits or offering pure entertainment.
We needed to remind them that Rice Krispies occupies a unique position between these two extremes.

Snap, crackle 'n' pop prompts delight and wonder among children.
So in 'Chairs' we created stories around those lovely things only kids do when Rice Krispies hits the breakfast table.
