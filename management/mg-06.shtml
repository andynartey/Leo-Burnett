Inspired by this, we defined a new Brand Purpose for Daz:
To make peoples' everyday lives a little bit, well, brighter

and created our very own Soap Opera - 'Cleaner Close'. Not only was this packed with the usual affairs, crime and adultery, it also provided a fabulous platform to talk about Daz, via a holistic media strategy with a remit to entertain rather than inform.

This turned our relationship with consumers from 'dull' to 'dazzling' with greater branded cut-through, higher advertising involvement, improved brand image ratings and significant brand switching, resulting in higher value share and a significant increase in ROI.
