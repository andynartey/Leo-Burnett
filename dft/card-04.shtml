Despite (and perhaps as a result of) being exposed to adult content
on the internet, in games and on multi-channel TV, research showed that children still had limited appreciation of the serious consequences of being involved in an accident.

To counter this, we created the 'Tales Of The Road' campaign - a series of cautionary stories designed to give the ritual of road safety new meaning and show that disregard for road safety can lead to major injury and emotional repercussions.

The campaign has created unprecedented awareness amongst
kids and has seen monthly web visits eclipse previous campaigns
by a margin of three to one.
