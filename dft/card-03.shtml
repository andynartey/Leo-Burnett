Roads are the single biggest killer of teenagers in the UK. But we found that simply asserting this fact did little to change behaviour.

If we were going to grab teenagers' attention and save lives,
we had to find a way to talk their language. We decided to let them
speak for themselves by creating an ad shot by teenagers for
teenagers on a mobile - an advertising first.

Within two weeks of its launch, over a third of teens in the UK had seen the ad. 95% of them said it made them think again about road safety.
