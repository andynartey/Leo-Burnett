Leo Burnett has acquired Holler, a leading digital content & social agency, which specialises in branded entertainment strategy, content creation and social.

Holler is a multiple-award winning agency, recently collecting 3 Gold IPA Effectiveness Awards including 'Best Innovation' for their work on E4's Skins.
