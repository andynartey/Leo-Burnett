Can you turn a wintry Carlisle railway station into a warm
and vibrant living space? It's a question we set out to discover
by doing it for real and then filming it.

Platforms became gardens, living rooms and kitchen areas.
Chandeliers and wallpaper adorned the station,
flooring was laid on bare concrete.

And was it a success? Passengers alighted from their trains
to marvel at the transformation, enjoying a wonderful moment
of 'home' before going on to their own homes.
A moment of magic,courtesy of Homebase, shared by almost
50,000 viewers on YouTube.
